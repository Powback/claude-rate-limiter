Done! I've created a complete Space Invaders game with:

**Features:**
- **Player ship** — Control with arrow keys (or WASD), shoot with spacebar
- **Scoring system** — 10 points × current wave per enemy destroyed
- **Lives** — Start with 3, lose one when hit by enemy fire
- **Wave progression** — Enemies increase in count and speed with each wave
- **Collision detection** — Player bullets hit enemies, enemy bullets hit player
- **Game over screen** — Shows final score and wave reached, restart with spacebar

**Gameplay:**
- Enemies move side-to-side and shoot randomly
- When all enemies are defeated, the next wave spawns with more enemies
- Enemies move down when hitting screen edges (classic style)
- Game ends if enemies reach the bottom or you lose all lives

Open `space-invaders.html` in any browser to play!
